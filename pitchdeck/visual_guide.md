# Visual & Image Suggestions for Pitch Deck - AI Legal Assistant

## Color Theme
- **Primary:** Navy blue (#1a237e)
- **Accent:** Gold (#ffd700), White (#ffffff)
- **Text:** Dark gray (#333333) on light backgrounds, white on dark
- **Style:** Professional, trustworthy, corporate legal aesthetic

## Slide 1: Title & Tagline
- **Visual:** Scales of justice icon with AI circuit pattern overlay. Clean navy background.
- **Template:** Logo center, tagline below, subtle legal pattern (gavels, books) in background.

## Slide 2: Problem
- **Visual:** Stack of expensive legal bills, person looking confused at legal documents, locked courthouse.
- **Template:** Left = impactful image, right = problem stats with red/orange accent.

## Slide 3: Solution
- **Visual:** Open network of AI nodes providing legal guidance. People accessing from phones/laptops globally.
- **Template:** Central network diagram with Bittensor logo, solution points around.

## Slide 4: Market Opportunity
- **Visual:** Bar chart showing $28B legal tech market. Growth projection arrow.
- **Template:** Large market size number center, supporting data points around.

## Slide 5: How It Works
- **Visual:** Horizontal flow: Legal Query -> Miner AI Models -> Advice + References -> Validator Verification -> Score -> Reward.
- **Template:** Flowchart with legal-themed icons (gavel, book, checkmark).

## Slide 6: Miner & Validator Design
- **Visual:** Two-column: Miner (AI analyzing legal docs) left, Validator (verifying references) right.

## Slide 7: Bittensor Integration

- **Template:** Architecture center, reward formulas as callout boxes.

## Slide 8: Competitive Advantage
- **Visual:** Comparison matrix with competitors (LegalZoom, ChatGPT, traditional firms).

## Slide 9: Go-to-Market

## Slide 10: Team & Contact
- **Visual:** Team photos/avatars with professional backgrounds, social icons.
